FAQs about Zelina Vega

Who is Zelina Vega?

Zelina Vega, born Thea Megan Trinidad Büdgen, is an American professional wrestler and actress. She is currently signed to WWE, where she performs on the Raw brand.

What is Zelina’s real name?

Her real name is Thea Megan Trinidad Büdgen.

How old is Zelina?

She was born on December 27, 1990, making her 33 years old as of 2024.

What are Zelina’s notable achievements in WWE?

She won the inaugural Queen’s Crown tournament in 2021 and the WWE Women’s Tag Team Championship with Carmella.

Who is Zelina Vega married to?

She is married to Dutch professional wrestler Tom Büdgen, known by his ring name Malakai Black, formerly Aleister Black in WWE.

Has Zelina appeared in movies or TV shows?

Yes, she appeared in the movie Fighting with My Family and served as a commentator in the video game Street Fighter 6.

What is Zelina’s height and weight?

She is 4 feet 11 inches tall and weighs around 114 pounds.

What was Zelina’s role in TNA (Total Nonstop Action Wrestling)?

In TNA, she performed under the ring name Rosita and won the TNA Knockouts Tag Team Championship with Sarita.

What led to Zelina’s release from WWE in 2020?

She was released after supporting wrestling unionization, which was linked to WWE’s policy on third-party platforms.

When did Zelina return to WWE after her 2020 release?

She returned to WWE on the July 2, 2021, episode of SmackDown.

What faction is Zelina currently a part of?

She is a member of the Latino World Order (LWO) after aligning with Legado Del Fantasma in 2022.
