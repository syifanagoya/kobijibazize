Beauty fans swear Superdrug's 4 hydrating serum is 'best on the high street'

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Superdrug shoppers have been left so impressed with the results of a hydrating serum that they claim that it is one of the best skincare products you can buy on the market.

The Superdrug Me+ Hyaluronic Booster, which is priced at just £4.49, has been snapped up by enthusiastic beauty fans who say that it leaves their skin "flawless" almost instantly and quicker than any other hyaluronic based product.

Hyaluronic acid is a key skincare superhero for anyone looking to reduce wrinkles and fine lines on the skin, and it looks like Superdrug's own-branded booster does just the job. Reviewers have remarked on how it "smoothed out" crows feet as "the best hyaluronic acid on the market".

Infused with 2% of the key skincare ingredient, this nifty booster is said to give your skin a boost of moisture and help with the appearance of dehydrated skin and leave it looking younger within just four weeks of use.

According to Superdrug, a self-assessment trial of 60 people who used the Me+ Hyaluronic Booster found that 98% of participants said that it left their skin feeling hydrated.

Even better then as the health and beauty retailer recently launched great savings in its Me+ range, with its Hyaluronic Booster reduced from £5.99 to £4.49. The five-star rated product is also included in Superdrug's 'buy one get second half price on selected Superdrug skincare and body' offer.

The booster has managed to amass hundreds of glowing give-star reviews on the Superdrug website as shoppers claim that it delivers quicker results than any other hyaluronic acid product on the market.

One positive shopper: "I've used a few hyaluronic products & most do a reasonable job of hydrating my skin. This is a little thicker than most ive used but does an amazing job of keeping my skin moisturised throughout the day, smoothing out my fine lines, crows feet & even my more deep set wrikles.

"My skin feels plumper, bouncier and soft to the touch. It is slightly thick & tacky upon application but does not drag & soaks in quite quickly, drying to a smooth velvet feel leaving it perfect for make up application. I'm really glad ive added this to my skin care routine as its made a real difference."

A second happy customer wrote: "Best high street HA [Hyaluronic Acid]. For me this beats any other HA currently on the market and believe me I have tried them all. It has given me quicker results and flawless skin within a shorter time frame than any other HA. I actually really love how the consistency is slightly thicker. For best results apply directly on to damp skin when you get out of the shower for example… I will 100% continue to use this product."
