A well-known American singer-songwriter, musician, author, Actor, and businessman, Jimmy Buffett is known for his music, combining folksy Southern rock with his obsession with the Caribbean.

Buffett was born in Pascagoula, Mississippi, on December 25, 1946.

He also spent time growing up in Mobile, Alabama. He started out as a country musician in Nashville, Tennessee, in the late 1960s, when he also cut his debut album, the folk-rock album with a country influence called Down to Earth.

He created his own style, a blend of country, rock, and pop with tropical and seaside themes, in the early 1970s.

Since then, Buffett has put out over 30 albums and sold 20 million records globally.

He is renowned for his entrepreneurial endeavors, which include owning restaurants, hotels, and a brand of beers.

One of the richest artists in the world, Buffett has a $1 billion net worth as of 2023.

He has organized fundraisers for Democratic candidates, including one for Hillary Clinton in 2016 and is a supporter of the Democratic Party.
