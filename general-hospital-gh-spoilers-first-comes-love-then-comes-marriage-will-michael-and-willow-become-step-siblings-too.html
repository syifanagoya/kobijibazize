First Comes Love, Then Comes Marriage Will Michael and Willow Become Step-Siblings Too?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 5, 2024

General Hospital (GH) spoilers reveal Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard) has asked Nina Reeves (Cynthia Watros) to be his wife, and heads are starting to turn at the uncomfortable news. Sonny may only be Michael Corinthos’ (Chad Duell) former stepfather, but he raised him and is the only father he’s ever really known. Willow Corinthos (Katelyn MacMullen) has only recently discovered that Nina is her birth mother, though she’s far from a mother figure in Willow’s eyes. Michael’s relationship with Sonny might be as strained as Willow and Nina’s is right now, but that doesn’t change the fact that Sonny and Nina’s nuptials would bring this family full circle — making Willow and Michael step-siblings or sorts. It’s not really too close for comfort in Port Charles, where everyone eventually swaps spouses, children, and lovers. But will the happy couple actually make it to saying I do?

General Hospital Spoilers — Michael Corinthos Won’t Be Celebrating

Michael isn’t keen on the idea of Sonny marrying anyone again, least of all — Nina. This woman has taken so much from his family, and now it feels like Sonny is glorifying all of that. What did Michael expect? Was Sonny supposed to hide his love for Nina in a cloak of shame? Well, yeah, sort of! Michael loathes Nina and he can’t stand that she now has attachments to the family through Willow, too. But that’s the whole point, isn’t it? Willow is the most important part of this for Michael, so he’s going to keep his mouth shut — for now.

General Hospital Updates – Willow Corinthos Is Forever The Neutral Party

Willow and Michael aren’t supposed to keep secrets from each other — even though he only recently seemed to get that memo. Willow is normally a pretty calm and collected person who only gets feisty when she’s pushed to the brink. Right now, she’s living on the brink, and she doesn’t have the energy to fight or boil herself in negative feelings. So, she’s going to accept Sonny and Nina’s engagement and wish them well. She wouldn’t want anything more or less from either of them, and they’ve only been happy for her and Michael.

Will Nina Reeves Actually Make It Down The Aisle?

Michael and Willow’s feelings really don’t matter, do they? Animosity might not make it any easier for Sonny and Nina to make it down the aisle, but it’s not likely that these two will be the parties holding them back. Rather, Nina has done that all by herself when she turned Carly Spencer (Laura Wright) into the SEC. It’s only a matter of time before she’s found out, and if we know Carly, her timing is going to be on point when she reveals it all to Sonny. Nina might make it down the aisle, but will someone stand up and object? Keep checking back with us for more GH news and spoilers just ahead to find out.
