What is the Net Worth Of Sophia Bush in 2024?

In 2024, Sophia Bush’s net worth is estimated at a robust $11 million. This figure reflects her successful career in television and film, notably through her roles in One Tree Hill and Chicago P.D..

To provide a comparison, Hilarie Burton, another prominent One Tree Hill actress, has a net worth of about $5 million, while Sarah Roemer, known for her role alongside Bush in Chicago P.D., stands around $2 million.

Bush’s net worth signifies her diverse income sources beyond acting, including directing, real estate investments, and brand endorsements.

Sophia Bush’s Career Achievements and Impact on Net Worth

Sophia Bush has been a prominent figure in Hollywood, with her acting career primarily contributing to her substantial net worth.

Beginning with her breakout role as Brooke Davis in One Tree Hill, she quickly became a household name. This role not only catapulted her to fame but also set her on the path to financial success.

Her transition to Chicago P.D. as Detective Erin Lindsay further solidified her status, attracting lucrative deals and opportunities. Her career achievements include winning a Teen Choice Award and the prestigious Vail Film Festival Rising Star Award.

Real Estate Investments and Their Contribution to Sophia Bush’s Wealth

Beyond the silver screen, Sophia’s savvy investments in real estate have also padded her portfolio. In 2015, she purchased a condo in Chicago for $1.6 million and sold it five years later for just under $2 million.

She also owns a home in the Hollywood Hills, acquired in the early 2010s for $1.4 million. These strategic investments reflect her smart financial acumen, significantly boosting her net worth.
