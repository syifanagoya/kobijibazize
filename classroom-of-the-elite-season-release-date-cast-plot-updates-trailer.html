Are you guys waiting for the “Classroom of the Elite” Season 3? Then it might be over friends! One of the best anime series among the audience is supposed to come back with the new volumes of the Japanese light novel “Manga”. 

If you don’t know about the “Classroom of the Elite,” let us take a quick look here. “Classroom of the Elite” is inspired by a Japanese light novel pen down by Shogo Kinugasa and illustrated by Shunsaku Tomas. It also got featured in a manga series called out under the direction of Youkoso Jitsuryoku in January 2016. It received so much fanbase due to its electrifying storyline. Later, three Manga volumes were selected for the “Classroom of the Elite” season 1, consisting of 12 episodes. Season 1 streamed on the 12th of July 2017 and run-up to September 2017. One of the best reasons which make it worth watching is to revive your dream school life and how your life takes a turn if you accompany your right people and beloved real friends. Classroom Of The Elite Season 3 Storyline:

The storyline of this popular anime series depicts the environment of Elite school, which not only makes you feel an academic hardship scenario but also how your life rolls outside the four walls of the classroom. The protagonist of the series Kiyotaka Ayanokoji seems like an introverted kid placed in D-class.

The government runs the school to improve their country by bringing out the best from their future generation. That’s why they divide sections A to D in terms of an individual’s student performance, which creates inferiority to make them better. Season 1 all goes around the transformation of class-D students to class-A.

Their motivation and healthy competition among each other make it worthwhile to watch. One can relate to the series’ characters as every school has some familiar students.

Classroom Of The Elite Season 2 Recap:

Ayanokouji intervenes to prevent Ryuuen from being expelled from school, aiming to eliminate another troublesome classmate who has been a source of annoyance for him. He goes on a date with Satou, during which she proposes to him. Eventually, it is revealed that he has been skillfully manipulating Karuizawa, molding her into his perfect pawn.

As he walks away from it all, Ibuki tries to persuade him not to give up and reminds him of his own words. However, Ryuuen appears disinterested in engaging in any more power struggles.

Classroom Of The Elite Season 2 Review:

Classroom of the Elite Season 2, also known as Youkoso Jitsuryoku Shijou Shugi no Kyoushitsue 2nd Season, has received mixed feedback from viewers. While some have praised its storyline and character development, others have expressed criticism towards its rushed pacing and lower quality animation compared to the first season.

According to a review on MyAnimeList, the main character Ayanokoji is described as “dark and mysterious, with a fascinating backstory” and consistently surprises viewers throughout the show. However, an Anime-Planet review states that the second season falls short when compared to the first, with a lackluster storyline and noticeably inferior animation. On Anime Ignite, another review points out that the first two arcs of the season felt rushed and bland in comparison to the light novel, but the last few episodes were highly enjoyable.

Overall, opinions on Classroom of the Elite Season 2 are divided. While some viewers appreciate its storyline and character development, others have reservations about its pacing and animation quality.
