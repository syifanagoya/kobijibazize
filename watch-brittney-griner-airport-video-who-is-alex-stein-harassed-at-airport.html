Alex Stein is a self-proclaimed comedian and troll who has gained attention as a right-wing social media personality.

Known for his controversial videos shared on Twitter and YouTube, Stein often sparks controversy with his provocative content.

His initial claim to fame came in 2012 when he appeared on the ABC reality TV show “The Glass House.” However, Stein transitioned into a provocateur over time.

In July 2022, Stein made headlines for harassing Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, a member of the United States House of Representatives, in a video that he shared on social media with his followers.

The video quickly went viral, generating significant attention and creating a storm of controversy.

Stein has also attracted attention for his involvement in a rap performance at a Texas City Council meeting, which gained traction and further contributed to his notoriety.

Overall, Alex Stein is a polarizing figure known for his controversial online presence and his ability to generate attention and stir up discussions through his videos and public appearances.

WNBA releases statement on Brittney Griner incident

In recent news, Alex Stein made headlines for harassing WNBA star Brittney Griner.

This incident has highlighted the need for creating measures to prevent similar situations and ensure the safety of WNBA players during their travels.

In response to the incident, the network released a statement affirming its commitment to the safety of Brittney Griner and all WNBA players.
