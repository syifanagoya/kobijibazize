What is Jackie Chan's net worth?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 26, 2024

LONG-TIME actor and comedian Jackie Chan has starred in some of the most iconic movies in history.

Chan is most known for his role as Lee in the Rush Hour film franchise.

What is Jackie Chan's net worth?

The Hong Kong-born actor has accumulated a massive amount of wealth through his acting and producing career in the entertainment industry.

According to Celebrity Net Worth Chan is estimated to be worth a total of $400million.

He has earned his multi-millions through working on many films as an actor, producer and stuntman.

Forbes also reports that the actor has his own line of movie theaters.

What movies has Jackie Chan been in?

Chan, who was born on April 7, 1954, has been acting for decades in the film industry.

Many know Chan from his character Lee in the Rush Hour film franchise.

There are total of three Rush Hour movies which were released between 1998 and 2007.

He acted alongside fellow actor and comedian Chris Tucker in the franchise.

Some of Chan's other notable films include:

  • Rumble In The Bronx (1995)
  • First Strike (1996)
  • Shanghai Noon (2000)
  • The Tuxedo (2002)
  • The Karate Kid (2010)
  • The Spy Next Door (2010)
  • Vanguard (2020)

He has been in countless movies with many other major actors such as Owen Wilson, Lucy Liu, and Jaden Smith.

Chan was listed on the Forbes Celebrity 100 list of 2020 ranking at number 80 on the prestigious list.

Chan reportedly made $40million in 2020 alone.

Is Jackie Chan married?

The acting veteran has been married since 1982 to wife Joan Lin.

Lin and Chan have a total of two kids together: Jaycee Chan and Etta Ng Chok Lam.

Jaycee has followed in his father's footstep somewhat as he also works in the entertainment industry.
