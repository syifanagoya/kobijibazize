Early Life

Andrew Garfield was born to Richard Garfield and Lynn on 20th August 1983. He was born in Los Angeles, California, but grew up in Epsom, Surrey. He was a swimmer and gymnast and attended the City of London Freemen’s School in Ashtead and Priory Preparatory School in Banstead. He, later on, he took up acting and graduated from the Central School of Speech and Drama in 2004.

He began dating ‘The Amazing Spider-Man’ co-star Emma Stone in 2011 but eventually broke-up in 2015.

Career

Garfield initially started his career by acting on stage but later on, appeared on TV in the comedy series ‘Sugar Rush’ in 2005. He also appeared in the British Sci-fi Tv series ‘Doctor Who’ in 2007. In 2007, he made his movie debut with the American war drama ‘Lions for Lambs’, in which he played a supporting role. His first major role in films was with ‘Boy A,’ where he played the lead role of a boy who is troubled to start a new life after he is released from jail.

Later he appeared in many movies like ‘The Other Boleyn Girl,’ ‘The Imaginarium of Doctor Parnassus, ‘ and ‘Never Let Me Go.’ He further played the lead role in the 2010 drama movie ‘The Social Network, ‘ which was a commercial success, and it also got nominated for the Oscars.

He became popular with the Marvel series of the 2012 superhero movie ‘The Amazing Spider-Man’ and also showed us his amazing acting skills in its sequel.

He later appeared in the lead role of the 2016 war drama film ‘Hacksaw Ridge.’ His performance as the lead role in the historical movie ‘Silence’ bagged positive reviews, though it was not a commercial success. The American neo-noir crime thriller ‘Under the Silver Lake’ is set to release in 2018 with Garfield as the lead role.

Awards & Achievements

Garfield, in 2008, won the BAFTA (British Academy Television Award) TV Award for Best Actor for the British film ‘Boy A.’ His appearance as a lead role in the 2016 war drama film ‘Hacksaw Ridge’ made him earn an Oscar nomination and was also the most successful film of the year.

He also won the AACTA Award for the Best Actor for the film ‘Hacksaw Ridge’ and also won an Oscar nomination for the same movie in the year 2016.
